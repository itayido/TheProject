🐒 CHIMP- TEST 🐒

Instructions

Test how many numbers you can remember and recall correctly. Each round, a number will appear
on the screen for a few seconds. Your goal is to memorize it and type it correctly after it disappears.

🕹️ How to play:

  1. Start the game.
    Press the "Start" or "Play" button to begin.
  2. Memorize the number.
    A number will appear on the screen for a short time. Try to memorize it carefully.
  3. Number disappears.
    The number will vanish, and you'll be asked to click it from memory.
  4. Check your number.
    If you're wrong, the game ends, and you'll see your score (the highest level you reached).

💡 Tips:

  • Focus and try to remember the number in chunks.
  • Stay calm — the numbers get longer gradually.
  • Challenge yourself to beat your previous high score!
